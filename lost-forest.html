LOST-FOREST

Lost Forest is a degnated forest created by the Burea of Land Management to protect an ancient stand of pondorosa pine in the remote high desert country of nothern Lake Country, in the South central area of the U.S state of Oregon. Lost Forest is an isolated area of pine trees seperated from the nearest ci=ontiguous forest land by forty miles of arid desert. Threre are no springs or surface water in Lost Forest, and much of the southwest portion of the natural area is covered by large shifting sand dunes that are slowly encroaching on the forest

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

You will find the Lost Forest in the Nothern Frontier.

Turn left at the entrance to the monkey trail
