TIGER

The Tiger is the largest living cat species and a member of the genus Phanthera. It is most recognisable for its dark vertical stripes on orange fur with a white underside. An apex predotor, it primarily preys on ungulates such as deer and wild boar.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT

Tigers can live in a range of environments, including the Siberian taiga, swamps, grasslands and rainforests
