RHINO

A Rhino is a member of the five extant species off odd-teod ungulates in the family Rhinocerotidae. Two of the extant species are native to Africa, and three to South and Southeast Asia.

Most wild African rhinos are now found in just four countries. South africa, Namibia, Zimbabwe and Kenya. We work to protect a number of their natural habits including, Mau-Mara-Serengeti and coastal Tanzania.They mainly roam grassland and open savannah.
